PRIMER RECONSTITUTION:

To reconstitute dry primers:

***Spin down primer tube first!!!***

100 µM = X nmoles lyophilized primer + (X × 10 µl molecular grade H2O) 

****IN THE KELLY LAB WE USUALLY KEEP STOCK PRIMERS AT 200uM.
To do this, make the above calculation, but cut the amount of water in half.

For example, if there are 38.2 nmol of primer then by adding 382 µl of H2O, a 100 µM primer stock is created.




PRIMER DILUTION:

To dilute 100µM primer stock to 10µM, add 10µL stock to 90µL water.

To dilute 200µM primer stock to 10µM, add 5µL stock to 95µL water.
