To make 1 liter of "Lysis Buffer" (add in numerical order):
1. 50 ml of 2 M Tris-HCL, pH 8.0
2. 200 ml of 0.5M EDTA, pH8.0. ** pH is critical! -- add NaOH to EDTA until pH reaches 8.0
3. 2 ml of 5M NaCl
4. up to 975 ml double-distilled water
5. 25 ml of 20% SDS (w/v)

Source: Longmire JL, Maltbie M, Baker RJ. Use of lysis buffer in DNA isolation and its implication for museum collections. Museum Texas Tech Univ. 1997;163.
